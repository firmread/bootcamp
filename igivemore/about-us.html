Our aim

To advance philanthropic giving by providing:

   (1) an engaging profile that motivates you to grow your impact by giving and raising more money for charity;

   (2) best-in-class social media applications that magnify your ability to disseminate a cause's story; and

   (3) actionable analytics that inform fundraising strategies.

iGiveMore is a web platform for charitable fundraising and corporate cause marketing campaigns.

For Individuals

Convenient

Track all your charitable giving and fundraising in one place: your iGiveMore profile.

Visual
See how you inspired your friends to donate in your personal giving graph.

Deeper Impact
Share using e-mail, Facebook, Twitter or Linkedin and inspire your friends to donate.

For Nonprofits

Social Media

Seamlessly integrate your fundraising with our Facebook app, e-mail and other social media (Twitter and Linkedin).

Smart Data
Improve your understanding of who is raising your money online.

Better Teams
Manage your fundraising team with fundraiser and donor rewards.

For Businesses

Maximize Value

Track your corporate donations and share your impact on the cause using our social media technology.

Easy Employee Giving
Setting up an employee giving program has never been easier or more affordable.

Mobile Marketing
Leverage a Foursquare charitable check-in campaign and show your customers how they are unlocking charitable donations with our Foursquare App.
