iGiveMore enables people to launch and visualize the viral growth of their online fundraising campaign. Individuals get an interactive visualization of how they inspired their friends to donate to a cause using e-mail, facebook, twitter, Linkedin. Organizations get an unparalleled understanding of who is raising money and what social media channels are most effective in raising money.

The Lead Developer oversee a small team of web developers and external development partners in Manhattan and abroad. This role will involve the development of tools for and aggregation of data across multiple platforms including Facebook, Twitter, websites and email so a broad technical skill set is required. The Lead Developer will be expected to synthesize cutting edge research on giving and leverage that knowledge to develop analytics platforms that assists charities in raising money. Experience developing Twitter and FaceBook Applications is a plus.

S/he must work well in small dynamic groups and be able to offer clear feedback on technical challenges. The individual should eagerly accept new challenges, adapt well to change, and learn quickly. Candidates should have a passion for making the world a better place and a belief that the Internet and social media can be a force for positive change.

 

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES

• Develop fundraising tools using Ruby on Rails.

• Manage and collaborate with outside development partners.

• Oversee the completion and integration of work by external developers

• Oversee development of front and back end of web applications.

• Offer technical solutions for improving the functionality of the services iGiveMore renders

 

EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE

• University or College degree in software engineering, computer science, or related area.

• Experience working in a start-up.

 

REQUIRED SKILLS

• Web development experience including thorough understanding of Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript

• Database experience including knowledge of SQL queries

• Ability to provide input into product functionality and technical approach

• Identify inaccurate, incomplete or flawed designs early in the design/development cycle with suggestions for alternative approaches

• Excellent technical skills along with continuous refinement in advanced programming techniques to increase speed of work while not compromising quality

• Excellent interpersonal and communication skills

 

 

PREFERRED SKILLS and KNOWLEDGE

• Javascript and JQuery

• Knowledge of FaceBook Twitter API or experience developing FaceBook or Twitter Applications a major plus

• Experience with mobile application development (Android/IOS)

• Knowledge of user interface design principles and practices

 

COMPENSATION & BENEFITS

• Salary Commensurate with Experience

• Significant opportunity for advancement

• Equity

 

TO APPLY

• E-mail resume and sample of work (electronic submission or URL) to Matt@igivemore.org
